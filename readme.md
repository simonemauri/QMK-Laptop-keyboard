# NSK-H360E (ITA)

## Keyboard Layout

### STM32 pinout
| rows        | 0   | 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   |
| ----------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| arduino pin | 2   | 4   | 5   | 6   | 7   |
| qmk pin     | D1  | D4  | C6  | D7  | E6  |

| columns     | 0   | 1   | 2   | 3   |
| ----------- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| arduino pin | 16  | 15  | A1  | A3  |
| qmk pin     | B5  | B4  | B3  | C3  |
